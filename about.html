About PR Metal ART

Precision, partnership, and industrial excellence since 2014

Our Story

PR Metal ART was founded in 2014 by Mr. Pankaj Jangid with a vision to deliver high-precision sheet metal manufacturing solutions for modern industries. Starting from a modest setup in Jaipur, the company has grown into a reliable manufacturing partner for businesses across India. With dedication, technical expertise, and a strong commitment to quality, PR Metal ART has built a reputation for delivering durable and precisely engineered metal products.

Under the leadership of Mr. Pankaj Jangid, the company specializes in CNC laser cutting, sheet metal fabrication, bending, welding, and powder coating services. Over the years, PR Metal ART has successfully completed hundreds of industrial fabrication projects, manufacturing EV charging cabinets, electrical control boxes, solar cable trays, pharmaceutical industry components, and various custom sheet metal products.

Today, our manufacturing facility in VKI Jaipur is equipped with advanced machinery and supported by skilled professionals who ensure precision, reliability, and quality in every product we manufacture. At PR Metal ART, we continue to focus on innovation, efficiency, and long-term partnerships with our clients, truly living by our promise — “Where Precision Meets Perfection.”

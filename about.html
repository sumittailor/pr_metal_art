About PR Metal ART

Precision, partnership, and industrial excellence since 2015

Our Story

Founded in Pune in 2015, PR Metal ART began with a single laser cutting machine and a vision to deliver world-class metal fabrication to Indian industries. Over the past decade, we've grown into a trusted partner for over 100 industrial clients, from automotive giants to infrastructure builders.

What sets us apart is our relentless focus on precision. Every sheet we cut, every component we bend, is inspected to tolerances as tight as 0.1mm. Our investment in the latest laser technology and a highly skilled team ensures that even the most complex bulk orders are delivered on time, every time.

Today, we operate from a 50,000 sq. ft. facility equipped with 5 high-power laser cutters, 8 CNC press brakes, and a dedicated quality lab. But our growth hasn't changed our core: we remain a family-run business that values long-term relationships over short-term gains.
